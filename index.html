Casey Fera grew up around artists and musicians. He has played in bands since high school, as a kid casey spent many hours manually editing videos with a combination of analog VCRs and CD players before the easy access of digital workstations. In college he experienced the switch over from linear to non linear editing.

 

He landed a job while still in film school at a local video production company and eventually started his own: Grand Illusion Entertainment.

 

Casey does a bit of everything, camera, editing, lighting, even a little web design. Currently Casey is most excited to debut his first feature documentary, Zuri' Pride, with plans in the future to do many more.
